Submission Guidelines

REVIZ Science Journal welcomes original research articles, reviews, and short communications across all scientific disciplines. All submissions undergo rigorous peer review to ensure the highest standards of scientific quality and integrity.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Original, previously unpublished research
  • Significant contribution to the field
  • Clear methodology and analysis
  • Adherence to ethical guidelines
  • Properly formatted manuscript

Article Types

Research Articles

Original research with complete methodology, results, and discussion (6,000-8,000 words).

Review Articles

Comprehensive surveys of specific research areas (8,000-10,000 words).

Short Communications

Brief reports of significant findings (2,000-3,000 words).

Methodology Papers

Novel experimental or computational methods (4,000-6,000 words).

Review Process

1

Initial Screening

Editorial office checks for completeness and adherence to basic guidelines (1-3 days).

2

Peer Review

Minimum two independent experts evaluate scientific quality (2-4 weeks).

3

Editor Decision

Editor-in-Chief makes final decision based on reviews (1 week).

Manuscript Formatting

Proper formatting ensures efficient processing of your manuscript. Please follow these guidelines carefully to avoid delays.

General Requirements

  • Microsoft Word (.docx) or LaTeX (PDF + source files)
  • 12 pt Times New Roman or Arial font
  • Double line spacing
  • 2.5 cm (1 inch) margins on all sides
  • Continuous line numbering
  • Page numbers in footer

Manuscript Structure

Title Page

Title, author names/affiliations, corresponding author details, abstract (200 words), keywords (5-8).

Main Text

Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion (for research articles).

References

Vancouver style, numbered sequentially as cited.

Figures & Tables

High resolution (300+ DPI), numbered sequentially with descriptive captions.

Supplementary Materials

Additional data, methods, or multimedia files submitted separately.

Reference Style Example

1. Smith AB, Jones CD. Novel approaches to quantum computing. Nature. 2022;605(7909):234-245. 
2. Lee EF, et al. CRISPR applications in medicine. N Engl J Med. 2021;385(12):1123-1134.
3. National Institutes of Health. Guidelines for clinical research. 2020. Available from: https://www.nih.gov/guidelines

Ethical Guidelines

REVIZ Science Journal is committed to upholding the highest standards of publication ethics. We adhere to the guidelines set by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE).

Authorship

  • All listed authors must have made significant contributions to the research
  • Ghost, guest, or gift authorship is prohibited
  • Corresponding author must verify all co-authors have approved the manuscript
  • Changes to authorship after submission require written consent from all authors

Originality & Plagiarism

All submissions are screened using iThenticate plagiarism detection software. Manuscripts with similarity scores above 15% (excluding references) may be rejected without review.

  • Submitted work must be original and not published elsewhere
  • Proper attribution must be given to all previously published material
  • Self-plagiarism (redundant publication) is not permitted

Conflicts of Interest

All authors must disclose any financial, personal, or professional relationships that could influence the research. This includes:

  • Funding sources (grants, sponsorships, etc.)
  • Employment or consulting relationships
  • Patent applications or ownership
  • Personal relationships with editors or reviewers

Human & Animal Studies

Important: Research involving human participants or animals must include ethics approval statements and comply with relevant guidelines (Declaration of Helsinki, NIH guidelines, etc.).

Data Availability

REVIZ supports open science and requires authors to:

  • Deposit data in appropriate repositories (e.g., Dryad, GenBank)
  • Include data availability statements in manuscripts
  • Share code and analysis scripts when applicable

Required Documents

The following must be submitted with your manuscript:

Ethics Approval Copyright Form Conflict of Interest Disclosure Author Contribution Statement
